Privacy Policy

elepaint Chrome Extension — Last updated: June 2026

elepaint is a browser extension for generating color palettes. It does not collect, store, or share any personal information or usage data. The clipboard permission is used solely to copy hex color codes when you click a swatch. No data ever leaves your browser.

Permissions

elepaint requests the clipboardWrite permission exclusively to copy a hex color code to your clipboard when you click a color swatch. No other data is read from or written to your clipboard.

Data collection

elepaint collects no analytics, no telemetry, no personal information, and no usage data of any kind. All color generation happens entirely within your browser using local JavaScript. Nothing is sent to any server.

Third parties

elepaint does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. It has no external dependencies.

## Full description (paste into the "Description" field)

```
elepaint generates professional color palettes in one click using the 60:30:10 rule — the same principle used by interior designers and brand strategists to create visually balanced color schemes.

Every palette is built in OKLCH, a perceptually uniform color space, which means the colors always feel balanced and intentional — not random or garish. You won't get muddy combinations or colors that look great individually but clash together.

─────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
─────────────────────────

Each palette gives you three colors with a clear role:

• Primary (60%) — light, calm tone for large areas like backgrounds and page fills
• Secondary (30%) — mid-tone for surfaces, cards, sidebars, and body text regions
• Accent (10%) — vivid, saturated color for buttons, links, highlights, and icons

─────────────────────────
HARMONY MODES
─────────────────────────

Choose from five color harmony types — or let elepaint pick one randomly:

• Analogous — neighboring hues on the color wheel; serene and cohesive
• Complementary — opposite hues; high contrast with visual tension
• Triadic — three evenly spaced hues; vibrant and balanced
• Split-Complementary — one hue plus the two neighbors of its opposite; dynamic but controlled
• Monochromatic — a single hue across three depths of lightness and saturation

─────────────────────────
FEATURES
─────────────────────────

✓ Click any swatch to instantly copy its hex code to your clipboard
✓ Hover a swatch to preview the hex code before copying
✓ Five harmony modes plus a Random mode
✓ Generates a fresh palette on every open — never shows you the same result twice
✓ Zero configuration, zero signup, zero data collection
✓ Works entirely offline — no internet connection required
✓ Lightweight: no external libraries, no tracking, no background processes

─────────────────────────
WHO IS THIS FOR?
─────────────────────────

• UI/UX designers who need a quick palette for mockups or design systems
• Web developers choosing a color scheme for a project
• Brand designers exploring color directions for a client
• Content creators who want visually consistent color choices for social media or video
• Anyone who wants colors that actually look good together without studying color theory

─────────────────────────
PRIVACY
─────────────────────────

elepaint collects zero data. No analytics, no telemetry, no account required. The clipboard permission is used only to copy hex codes when you click a swatch. Everything runs locally in your browser.
```
